Islaam: Its opposite is Kufr (disbelief). The actions of a disbeliever are not accepted (by Allaah), regardless of what good deed he may perform. The proof for this is Allaah's saying:

It is not for the polytheists to maintain the mosques of Allaah while they witness against their own selves of disbelief. The works of such are in vain and in Hell will they abide forever

And Allah's saying:

And We shall turn to whatever deeds they (polytheists) did and make such deeds as scattered floating particles of dust
