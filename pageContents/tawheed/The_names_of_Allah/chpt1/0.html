Ar-Rabb is the owner who has full authority over his property. Ar-Rabb, linguistically means, the master or the one who has the authority to lead. All of these meanings are correct for Allah. It was reported that Ar-Rabb is Allah's Greatest Name.

When it is alone, the word Rabb is used only for Allah. As for other than Allah, it can be used to say Rabb Ad-Dar, the master of such and such object.

  1. Tafsir Ibn Kathir, Al-Fatihah
