'Aaishah said:

كَانَ النَّبِيُّ صلى الله عليه وسلم يُعْجِبُهُ التَّيَمُّنُ فِي تَنَعُّلِهِ وَتَرَجُّلِهِ وَطُهُورِهِ وَفِي شَأْنِهِ كُلِّهِ‏

Rasoolullaah (pbuh) used to like to begin with the right in putting on shoes, combing his hair, in purification and in all of his affairs.

Abu Hurayrah narrates that the Prophet (pbuh) said:

إِذَا لَبِسْتُمْ وَإِذَا تَوَضَّأْتُمْ فَابْدَءُوا بِأَيَامِنِكُمْ

When you dress and when you make wudhoo, then begin with the right.

  1. Sahih al-Bukhari 168
  2. Abu Daawood (4141), at-Tirmidhee (1766), an-Nasaa.ee (402)
