From 'Abdullaah ibn 'Amr - about the way of performing wudhoo - he said:

ثُمَّ مَسَحَ بِرَأْسِهِ فَأَدْخَلَ إِصْبَعَيْهِ السَّبَّاحَتَيْنِ فِي أُذُنَيْهِ وَمَسَحَ بِإِبْهَامَيْهِ عَلَى ظَاهِرِ أُذُنَيْهِ

Then he pbuh wiped his head and entered his two forefingers into his ears and wiped the backs of his ears with his thumbs.

  1. Sunan Abi Dawud 135
