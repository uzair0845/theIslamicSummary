'Uthmaan (radhi-yAllaahu 'anhu) narrated:

أَنَّ رَسُولَ اللَّهِ تَوَضَّأَ فَخَلَّلَ لِحْيَتَهُ

that the Prophet (pbuh) used to run his (wet fingers) through his beard.

Some of the scholars have declared that entering water through the beard is obligatory and say: If he deliberately leaves it - then he must repeat the ( wudhoo and) prayer. And this is the saying of Imaam Ishaaq and Abu Thaur.

Most of the scholars however are of the opinion that the order is an order of desirability (istihbaab) and it is not obligatory - and that it is clearer that that which is obligatory is moving water through that part of the beard..so that it reaches the skin underneath.

Imam Ahmad and Layth and most of the scholars hold that moving water through the beard is obligatory when taking ghusl from Janaabah - and not obligatory in wudhoo.

  1. Ibn Maajah (no.430)
