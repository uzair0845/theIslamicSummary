The face (wajh) is determined as being everything between the beginnings of the hair down to the cheeks and the chin, and up to the start of the ears including that which is between the beard and ear.

Allaah Ta'ala says:

O Ye who believe! When ye prepare for prayer, wash your faces, and your hands (and arms) to the elbows; rub your heads (with water); and (wash) your feet to the ankles

Humraan ibn Abaan narrates that 'Uthmaan (radhi-yAllaahu 'anhu) called for water to make wudhoo and so mentioned the way in which the Prophet pbuh made wudhoo. Humraan said:

ثُمَّ غَسَلَ وَجْهَهُ ثَلاَثَ مَرَّاتٍ

Then he washed his face three times.

  1. Saheeh Muslim Book 2, Hadith
