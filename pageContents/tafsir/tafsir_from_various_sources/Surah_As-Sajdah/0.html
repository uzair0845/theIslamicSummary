Introduction

Other names of this surah:

  1. Surah Alif Laam Meem
  2. سورة التَنْزِيل
  3. Surah At-Tanzil As-Sajdah
  4. Surah Alif Laam Meem Tanzil
  5. Surah Alif Laam Meem Tanzil As-Sajdah
  6. سورة الجُرُز
  7. سورة المضاجِع
  8. سورة المُنجِية

Verse 27 mentions the word الجُرُز, verse 6 mentions المضاجِع and verse 2 mentions تَنْزِيل. Something having many names indicates its status.

Ibn Qayyim says that its recommended to read السجدة and الإنسان on Friday fajr prayer. He says these two surahs are legislated to remind the ummah what's going to happen on The Day of Judgement.
