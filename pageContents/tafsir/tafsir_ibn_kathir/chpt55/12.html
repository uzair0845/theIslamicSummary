The first two are for the near believers (Muqarribin) and the latter two are for those on the right (Ashab Al-Yamin). Abu Musa commented, "There are two gardens made of gold for the Muqarribin and two gardens made of silver for Ashab Al-Yamin."

Ibn Abbas said, "Mudhammatan means, they have become dark green because of extensive water irrigation." Muhammad bin Kab said: "Full of greenery." There is no doubt that the former two gardens are better, their branches are fresh, youthful and intermingling.

Ali bin Abi Talhah reported from Ibn Abbas: "It means gushing. And the free flowing is stronger than gushing." Ad-Dahhak said that it means, they are full of water and constantly gushing.

It was also said that Khayrat is plural of Khayrah and it is a righteous, well-mannered, beautiful woman, according to the majority of scholars. Al-Bukhari recorded that Abdullah bin Qays said that the Messenger of Allah said,

إِنَّ فِي الْجَنَّةِ خَيْمَةً مِنْ لُؤْلُؤَةٍ مُجَوَّفَةٍ، عَرْضُهَا سِتُّونَ مِيلًا، فِي كُلِّ زَاوِيَةٍ مِنْهَا أَهْلٌ، مَا يَرَوْنَ الْاخَرِينَ يَطُوفُ عَلَيْهِمُ الْمُؤْمِنُون

Verily, in Paradise, the believer will have a tent from a hollow pearl, the width thereof is sixty miles. In each corner of it there are wives for the believer that do not see the other wives, and the believer will visit them all.

Ali bin Abi Talhah reported from Ibn Abbas: "Rafraf means cushions." Mujahid, Ikrimah, Al-Hasan, Qatadah, Ad-Dahhak and others also said that Rafraf means cushions. Al-Ala' bin Badr said: "The Rafraf are arrayed hanging over the couches." Ibn Abbas, Qatadah, Ad-Dahhak and As-Suddi said that Abqariy means rich carpets

Abdullah bin Abbas said that 'Dhil-Jalal wal-Ikram' means, the Owner of greatness and pride. In a Hadith, the Prophet said,

إِنَّ مِنْ إِجْلَالِ اللهِ إِكْرَامُ ذِي الشَّيْبَةِ الْمُسْلِمِ، وَذِي السُّلْطَانِ، وَحَامِلِ الْقُرْآنِ غَيْرِ الْغَالِي فِيهِ، وَلَا الْجَافِي عَنْه

Verily, among the acts of venerating Allah, are honoring the elderly Muslims, the one in authority, and carrier (memorizer) of the Qur'an who avoids extremism and laziness with it.
