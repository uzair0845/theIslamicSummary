وَٱلسَّمَآءَ بِنَآءً۬

And the sky as a canopy

meaning, 'a ceiling'.

وَأَنزَلَ لَڪُم مِّنَ ٱلسَّمَآءِ مَآءً۬

And sends down for you water (rain) from the sky

meaning, through the clouds, when they need the rain. Hence, Allah caused the various types of vegetation and fruits to grow as a means of sustenance for people and their cattle.

فَلَا تَجۡعَلُواْ لِلَّهِ أَندَادً۬ا وَأَنتُمۡ تَعۡلَمُونَ

Then do not set up rivals unto Allah (in worship) while you know (that He alone has the right to be worshipped)

The Two Sahihs record that Ibn Mas'ud said, "I said to the Messenger of Allah pbuh, 'Which evil deed is the worst with Allah' He said,

أَنْ تَجْعَلَ للهِ نِدًّا وَهُوَ خَلَقَك

To take an equal with Allah, while He alone created you.
