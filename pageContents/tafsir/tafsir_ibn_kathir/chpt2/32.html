وَإِن ڪُنتُمۡ فِى رَيۡبٍ۬

And if you (Arab pagans, Jews, and Christians) are in Rayb

meaning, doubt.

مِّمَّا نَزَّلۡنَا عَلَىٰ عَبۡدِنَا

Concerning that which We have sent down (i.e. the Qur'an) to Our servant

meaning, Muhammad pbuh.

فَأۡتُواْ بِسُورَةٍ۬ مِّن مِّثۡلِهِۦ

then produce a Surah (chapter) the like thereof

meaning, similar to the Qur'an.
