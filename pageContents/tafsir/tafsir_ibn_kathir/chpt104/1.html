بِسْمِ اللَّهِ الرَّحْمَـنِ الرَّحِيمِ

Al-Hammaz refers to (slander) by speech, and Al-Lammaz refers to (slander) by action. This means that the person finds fault with people and belittles them.

Mujahid said, "Al-Humazah is with the hand and the eye, and Al-Lumazah is with the tongue."

Allah says,

Who has gathered wealth and counted it.

meaning, he gathers it piling some of it on top of the rest and he counts it up. Muhammad bin Kab said, "his wealth occupies his time in the day, going from this to that; then when the night comes he sleeps like a rotting corpse."

He thinks that his wealth will make him last forever.

meaning, he thinks that gathering wealth will make him last forever in this abode (the worldly life).

Verily, he will be thrown into Al-Hutamah.

meaning, the person who gathered wealth and counted it, will be thrown into Al-Hutamah, which is one of the descriptive names of the Hellfire. This is because it crushes whoever is in it.
