أَمْ تَسْأَلُهُمْ خَرْجاً

Or is it that you ask them for some Kharj

Al-Hasan said, "A reward." Qatadah said, "Some payment."

فَخَرَاجُ رَبِّكَ خَيْرٌ

But the recompense of your Lord is better

means, you are not asking for any wages or payment or anything for calling them to right guidance, rather you are hoping for a great reward from Allah.
