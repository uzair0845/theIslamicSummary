Then, after them, We created other generations.

meaning, nations and peoples.

ثُمَّ أَرْسَلْنَا رُسُلَنَا تَتْرَى

Ibn Abbas said, "(This means) following one another in succession."

فَأَتْبَعْنَا بَعْضَهُمْ بَعْضاً

means, 'We destroyed them,'

وَجَعَلْنَـهُمْ أَحَادِيثَ

meaning, stories and lessons for mankind.
