Tawhid Ar-Rububiyyah

Allah mentioned,

إِيَّاكَ نَعْبُدُ

before,

وَإِيَّاكَ نَسْتَعِينُ

because the objective here is the worship, while Allah's help is the tool to implement this objective. Certainly, one first takes care of the most important aspects and then what is less important, and Allah knows best.
