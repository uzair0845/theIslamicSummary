Seeking Refuge With Allah When One Is Angry

In his Musnad, Al-Hafiz Abu Yala Ahmad bin Ali bin Al-Muthanna Al-Mawsili reported that Ubayy bin Kab said, "Two men disputed with each other in the presence of the Messenger of Allah and the nose of one of them became swollen because of extreme anger. The Messenger of Allah said,

إِنِّي لَأَعْلَمُ شَيْئًا لَوْ قَالَهُ لَذَهَبَ عَنْهُ مَا يَجِدُ: أَعُوذُ بِاللهِ مِنَ الشَّيْطَانِ الرَّجِيمِ

I know of some words that if he said them, what he feels will go away, 'I seek refuge with Allah from the cursed Satan.'

An-Nasa'i also recorded this Hadith in his book.
