Allah's statement:

It is a truthful promise

وإن الدين

meaning the Recompense.

لواقع

meaning it will surely come to pass

Ibn Abbas said:

ذات البهاء والجمال والحسن والاستواء

Full of beauty, grace, magnificence and perfection.

Mujahid, Ikrimah, Sa'id bin Jubayr, Abu Malik, Abu Salih, As-Suddi, Qatadah, Atiyah Al-Awfi, Ar-Rabi bin Anas and others said similarly. Ad-Dahhak, Al-Minhal bin Amr and others said:

مثل تجعد الماء والرمل والزرع إذا ضربته الريح، فينسج بعضه بعضًا طرائق، فذلك الحبك

The meandering of the water, sand and plants when the wind passes over them; carving paths out of them, that is the Hubuk.
