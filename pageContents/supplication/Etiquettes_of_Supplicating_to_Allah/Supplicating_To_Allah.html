وَقَالَ رَبُّكُمُ ادْعُونِي أَسْتَجِبْ لَكُمْ ۚ إِنَّ الَّذِينَ يَسْتَكْبِرُونَ عَنْ عِبَادَتِي سَيَدْخُلُونَ جَهَنَّمَ دَاخِرِينَ

And your Lord said: 'Supplicate to Me and I will respond to you. Verily, those who disdain My worship will surely enter Hell in humiliation.'

وَإِذَا سَأَلَكَ عِبَادِي عَنِّي فَإِنِّي قَرِيبٌ ۖ أُجِيبُ دَعْوَةَ الدَّاعِ إِذَا دَعَانِ ۖ فَلْيَسْتَجِيبُوا لِي وَلْيُؤْمِنُوا بِي لَعَلَّهُمْ يَرْشُدُونَ

And when My slaves ask you [O Muhammad] concerning Me, then inform them that I am indeed near. I respond to the invocations of the supplicant when he calls on Me. So let them obey Me and believe in Me, so that they may be led aright.

أَمَّن يُجِيبُ الْمُضْطَرَّ إِذَا دَعَاهُ وَيَكْشِفُ السُّوءَ وَيَجْعَلُكُمْ خُلَفَاءَ الْأَرْضِ ۗ أَإِلَٰهٌ مَّعَ اللَّهِ ۚ قَلِيلًا مَّا تَذَكَّرُونَ

Is He not best, the One who responds to the desperate one when he calls upon Him and removes evil and makes you inheritors of the earth? Is there a deity with Allāh? Little do you remember.

Allah's Messenger pbuh said:

Du'a (supplication) is worship. Your Lord stated:

ادْعُونِي أَسْتَجِبْ لَكُمْ

Supplicate to Me and I will answer you.

Allah's Messenger pbuh said:

There is not a Muslim who supplicates to Allah for that which contains no sin, nor cutting family ties, except that Allah will grant him one of three things: Either He will hasten for him what he supplicated, or He will store it for him in the Hereafter, or He will avert from him its equivalent of evil.

They said: "What if we increase [in du'a]?" He replied:

With Allah is even more!
